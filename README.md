## About

AutoVault is a full-stack mobile app that connects car buyers and sellers in one clean, intuitive marketplace. Built as a complete product — from Google OAuth login to image uploads, live location, and featured listings — it handles the full buying and selling flow end to end.

The idea came from how cluttered and untrustworthy traditional car classifieds feel. AutoVault gives sellers a guided listing experience and buyers a clean, filtered browse with direct contact options — no middlemen, no noise.

---

## Features

### For Buyers
- Browse and filter cars by make, type, price, and location
- Like and save cars to revisit later
- View full specs, high-res gallery, and seller details on each listing
- See the car's location on an interactive map
- Contact the seller instantly via call, WhatsApp, or email

### For Sellers
- List a car through a guided 5-step form
- Upload a main photo plus up to 8 gallery images
- Auto-detect or manually pin your location
- Manage your listings — feature, edit, or delete them
- Feature your car to appear on the homepage carousel

## What I Built

This project started as a simple mobile app creation to make my first mobile app. Everything was custom-built or heavily modified — the data models, the listing flow, the contact system, the featured cars logic, and the UI.

Key things I implemented : the multi-step sell-car form with image upload to Appwrite Storage, the featured/unfeature system with real-time home feed updates via global state, per-feature SVG icons for car specs, Google OAuth with persistent sessions, and full CRUD on listings including gallery cleanup.
